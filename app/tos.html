By participating, Contestants agree to be bound by these Official Rules and the decisions of the judges and/or Sponsor, which are binding and final on matters relating to this Contest.

  1. CONTEST PERIOD. The Contest begins at 11:59 p.m. Greenwich Mean Time (“GMT”) on May 3, 2018 (the “Contest Start Date”) and ends at 11:59 p.m. GMT on May 21, 2018 (the “Contest Period”). These times are according to Sponsor’s time clock, which is set at Eastern Daylight Time. All entries must be submitted during the Contest Period. The Contest begins on the Contest Start Date; videos uploaded prior to the Contest Start Date are not eligible.

 

  1. SUBMISSIONS. Your entry is a reenactment of up to four (4) selected Measure Up Challenges (defined below in paragraph 4(b)) posted for replication inside the App during the Contest Period (“Submission”).

  1. To participate in the Contest, Contestants must download the App and become a registered user. If a Contestant is not a registered user of the App, Contestants must first download the App and register as a user within the App. Contestants will be asked to provide their name, provide and validate their email address, and create a password. In order to register, Contestants must also agree to HEED’s Terms of Use, Cookies Policy and Privacy Policy. Registration is free.

Sponsor will announce and post these selected Measure Up Challenges on the “Measure Up” tab within the App. Contestants can access and view the selected Measure Up Challenges uploaded specifically for the Contest. Using a video application or camera on Contestant’s smartphone, Contestant must recreate the selected Measure Up Challenge to the best of their ability and upload the created or existing video content from their smartphone to the App.  The Submission length should be no greater than 15 seconds -- if the Submission is longer, Sponsor’s software will judge only the first 15 seconds for the HEED Pro-Score prize (defined in paragraph 6 below).  If longer than 20 seconds, Sponsor may disqualify the Submission.

  3. Once the Submission is created, Contestants may access The App, upload the Submission as directed in connection with the Measure Up Challenge, and receive one entry into the Contest.

No negative or otherwise disparaging images (no images of anyone other than Contestant), comments or posts, please. We will remove any posts that, in the sole discretion of Sponsor, are inappropriate. Maximum four (4) entries per natural person (one per selected Measure Up Challenge). Maximum 1 (one) prize per natural person.

Submissions must be received no later than May 21, 2018 at 11:59 p.m. GMT according to Sponsor’s time clock. No other methods of delivery will be accepted.   1. SUBMISSION GUIDELINES AND CONTENT RESTRICTIONS.

Submission Guidelines: Your Submission can be as creative as you’d like within the following guidelines:

  • Submissions should be no longer than 15 seconds. If longer, they will not be scored beyond the first 15 seconds.
  • Submissions must be original recreations of the chosen Measure Up Challenges posted in the App for the Contest.
  • Submissions may not be altered once uploaded, but Contestants may submit a substitute video for the same Measure Up Challenge before the close of the Contest Period. Only the last video uploaded to the App by a Contestant for any Measure Up Challenge will be considered Contestant’s Submission for that Challenge.
  • Submissions should be clearly filmed, and mimic as closely as possible the selected Measure Up Challenge.
  • Submissions should be shot at an angle as close to the angle of the Measure Up Challenge as possible.

Except where prohibited by law, the following Content restrictions, apply:

  • Submissions must be original works of authorship, orchestrated by the person who enters the Submission. The video must not include third-party copyrighted material or artwork, without the copyright holder’s permission. Please, no music.  By entering, Contestants warrant that the Submission does not infringe any third party’s rights, and that Contestants have obtained any necessary permissions from all relevant third parties to enter the Contest.

  • Contestants hereby agree to indemnify Sponsor against any and all claims from any third party for any use by Sponsor of the Submission.
  • Although Sponsor is not reviewing Submissions for copyright or trademark violations, if, in the sole discretion of Sponsor, it is believed the Submission constitutes or may constitute copyright or trademark infringement, the Submission will be disqualified.
  • Sponsor reserves the right to exclude any Submission that it believes, in its sole discretion, doesn’t meet this criteria, and to delete any such Submission from any and all location(s) under Sponsor’s control.
  • No Submission shall portray Sponsor or any third party in a negative light.
  • Please, do not use the Submission to launch personal attacks on anyone or any discernable product, including competitor products.

There will be two (2) winners for each Measure Up Challenge, one Fan Favorite/People’s Choice winner and one HEED Technical winner, each judged differently as described below.  All Submissions will be evaluated through Sponsor’s proprietary similarity scoring algorithm and software (“HEED Pro-Score”) to measure the technical similarity and determine whether the Submission meets the Submission criteria, but there are two distinct ways to win.
  1. The Fan Favorite/People’s Choice winner for each Measure Up Challenge will be determined by public voting, and registered users of the App will be able to vote (up to one time per Submission) for their favorite video, by following the instructions contained in the App during the Contest Period. While the winner of the Fan Favorite/People’s Choice prize will be featured by Sponsor in some manner (for example, within the App, on the Sponsor’s website, in a live event venue or broadcast) there is no monetary prize.  Public voting should be based upon the Submission’s visual merit. 

  2. The HEED Technical winner, will be determined by HEED’s proprietary similarity scoring algorithm and software program, which measures the angle and technical similarity of the Submission to the original Measure Up Challenge. The Submission with the highest HEED Pro-Score, as determined by Sponsor’s software, will be deemed the winner.  In the event the same person received the highest score for two unique Submissions, s/he will be awarded the prize where his/her score is the highest and be disqualified from the other Measure Up Challenge(s). Judging will be done on or about one (1) week after the close of the Contest Period, but no later than May 31, 2018.
